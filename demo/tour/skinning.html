Changing How TinyMCE Looks via a Custom Skin

You can use custom skins to change the look of the TinyMCE to fit seamlessly with your application.

Click on the Light Mode / Dark Mode toggle to switch to the dark mode skin.

Use the skin builder to make your own skin. There are over 290 different variables you can customize.

Custom skins are located in the /src/xxx directory.

The skin configuration option points TinyMCE to the custom skin. You can also deploy the skin in a separate location and access the skin via the skin_url configuration option.
